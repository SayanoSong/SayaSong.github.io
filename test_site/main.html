Event Info

The Virtual Band Expo has announced its majestic return in 2022, with a brand new venue, dates, and an updated lineup. Slated for August 12, 13, 14th 2022, the festival will be debuting at Burl Event Grounds for its 5th annual edition !!!

The Virtual Band Expo is an annual music festival organized by Saya Song Media with many online singers who uses a virtual avatar generated using computer graphics. The performers of the Virtual Band Expo in each year might be changed.
